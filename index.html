About Me

Hi, I am Shreyansh. I am a Data Analyst who enjoys digging into messy datasets and turning them into stories that make sense. Over the past few years, I have worked with SQL, R, and Power BI to build dashboards, automate reports, and uncover insights: from streamlining operations at JSW Steel to exploring environmental data at EPA Victoria.
